Please read and follow the guidelines presented here within the Code of Conduct. By logging into the chat or accessing any associated mailing lists or forums/messages boards you are acknowledging that you are fully aware of the information presented here.

From this point forward Wanton Wicked may be referred to as “WW" (yeah, we've heard the WW for White Wolf bit :P).
From this point forward Wanton Wicked Site Administration, Storytellers and Cell Storytellers may simply be communally referred to as “moderators”.

Wanton Wicked, reserves the right, but does not assume the responsibility, to restrict communication which it deems in its discretion to be harmful to individual players, in violation of Wanton Wicked's rights or any third-party rights.

Prohibited chat room, forum and e-mail list actions (but are not limited to):

  • Profanity, vulgarity, or explicit content
    • Please try to keep public out of character rooms to PG-13 language (no f-bombs). Occasional swearing is tolerated but please curb your language if asked (by player or moderator). Also, over heavy sexual or violent conversations should be taken to private messages and not talked about openly for all to see.
  • Hate speech (e.g. racial, ethnic, sexist and religious slurs)
  • Promotion of or engaging in certain illegal activities
    • Specifically: Drugs, drug paraphernalia, computer hacking, copyright violations, rape or solicitation of a minor. Keep it clean and common sense.
  • Impersonation of another person, Wanton Wicked Administrator, Storyteller or Cell Storyteller by either screen name or self-representation
    • E.g. faking a screen name by using similar-looking characters, "spoofing" speech in a char room or claiming to hold an official title or position relating to the site.
  • Harassment of another player
    • E.g. flaming or continued personal attacks on the chat (or forum / mailing list) and repeated out of character cursing at one or a group of individuals.
  • Spamming through repeated posts, or off-topic content by word of intent to the chat, forum or lists
    • E.g. scrolling, flooding, polling or by "bumping" a forums post more than once in 24 hours.
  • Advertising a business, commercial or other gaming site
    • Advertising for White Wolf Studios is acceptable as is discussing places to buy, sell or trade books legally. Players are welcome to discuss the various official White Wolf chats but are in no way to bash or slander them or Wanton Wicked while doing so. Slandering another online game (be that in forum, chat or mailing list) will result in a Lv 4 Disciplinary Action.
  • Attempts to register a banned login name or a variation of a banned name, or declarations under a new screen name that you are "really" the banned name or attempts to re-subscribe to  any medium associated with Wanton Wicked after being banned or suspended.
  • Participating in account scams, which include, but is not limited to:
    • soliciting account information (passwords, personal information, etc.) whether by char, forum or email
    • downloading, posting for download, creating or using any kind of mod or cheat program or otherwise hack the Wanton Wicked Chat
  • All account names and character names must conform to all the above Code of Conduct rules, additionally names:
    • must not include protocol prefixes and domain suffixes (e.g. http://, .org, .com), be a reference to a business or other game, or be constructed as an advertisement
    • must not harm the reputation of any player or moderator by intent or effect
    • most not be or reference offensive or notorious persons, controversial political figures or cultural tragedies
    • must not contain discernibly inappropriate abbreviations or acronyms
    • at this time all Wanton Wicked character names should be in English text
  • Off-topic posts or casual chat/IC play in the wrong chat rooms or forums
    • out of character chat in a designated in character room
      • while not a major violation this should be kept to a bare minimum
      • use private messages or other IM programs (AIM, ICQ, etc) to carry on OOC chatter
    • combat play in non-combat rooms without a proper moderator
    • casual chat or free-form role-play in designated structured rules/dice-based game rooms
    • at Wanton Wicked discretion, off-topic chat or posts in topic specific rooms/forums

Disciplinary Guidelines

These disruptive behaviors are grounds for removal; the moderators are the final judges of whether a player's behavior qualifies as inappropriate or disruptive.

In other words, treat other people as you would wish to be treated. IC swearing and fights are fine as long as they stay IC and you act decent OOC.

The moderators will first warn a player if they are in violation of the Code of Conduct (also known as “CoC”). Warnings are just that, warnings. No serious disciplinary action may occur for letting a simple swear out in the OOC, below is a more structured look on disciplinary levels.

·Level One: Warning, either in the chat or via email. Documenting this with screen shots or saving the email(s). Place a note in the Storyteller Notes section for the character.

·Level Two: If the player repeats what they’ve already warned not to do then they will be docked experience points on the offending character. Leave note in the Storyteller Notes section for the character.

·Level Three: If the player is at a third warning then desanctioning of the character involved should be done.

·Level Four: A fourth level infraction will result in your account being placed on hold and all characters desanctioned for the interim. You may or may not get your account back and you may or may not get your characters back.

·Level Five: A fifth level infraction is something very serious as it constitutes a banning from the chat. This can be caused by a player continuously meta-gaming (and could go from Lv 1 to Lv 5) or someone threatening physical harm/stalking another or abusing a moderator. The Site Administration along with the Head Storyteller will have final say in all bannings (here's hoping we don't have any!).

These guidelines exist to help the moderators associated with the Wanton Wicked Chat establish a “fair for all” system of handling disciplinary action. The moderators are free to conduct stronger or lesser punishment as they deem fit. These guidelines do not exist to hinder the moderators.
